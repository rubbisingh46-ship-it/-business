Hello — I'm Nam Tushar Selal, a historian and archaeologist turned guide. With deep roots in the Dharchula–Pithoragarh region and years of experience leading cultural and pilgrimage journeys, I curate safe, meaningful, and comfortable trips to temples, valleys and alpine shrines. My background in history and archaeology brings richer context to every visit.

Ideal for cultural immersion and as a base for higher treks toward Aadi Kailash & Om Parvat.
